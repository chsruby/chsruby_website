Come Join Us on Thursday, April 8, 2010, at 6:30pm

We have regular meetings on the second Thursday of each month. The next meeting's location is still being worked out, but check back for updates.

Agenda ideas include:

  • Upcoming announcements
  • A presentation by Paul Brannan on Ruby blocks
  • Open source contribution
  • Promotion of Ruby locally
  • Development of www.charlestonruby.org and our other projects

There is no charge, and meetings are informal.
